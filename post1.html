Monday, April 16, 2007

First post

Welcome to my GSoC blog! GSoC 2007 is on, and I'm really excited to be part of it. I'm going to spend most of my summer holidays fixing bugs in Vim ("Vi IMproved"), which is a great text editor first released by Bram Moolenaar in 1991. It's not that Vim has many bugs -- some of its many features just need a bit of polishing. You can find the "Fix bugs" project idea here and my application abstract here.
